# Elide Status — 2026-06-17

Progress since [2026-06-10](status-2026-06-10.md).  One week,
~40 PRs (#557–#595).

The attestation design contract settled in the previous status
became running code, then a mint crypto-hardening sweep, then
mint moved out of the tree into its own repository.

---

## Major changes

### Volume-ownership attestation implemented

The claim-first contract (#555) landed end-to-end (#557–#574):

- `NameRecord` gains `latest_snapshot` (schema v3); fork basis
  now resolves from the names record rather than a separate read
  (#557/#558).
- Claim ordering pinned: `early_rebind` is control-plane only,
  `volume claim --force` is ownership-first recovery, the forced
  CAS is the cut read as a single head delta, and reap is gated
  on `names/<name>` still binding this fork (#559–#562).
- The synthesis path is retired — every manifest is
  volume-signed (#563).  Remote `start` anchors on the key
  shadow, the owned anchor threads through every `volume-ro`
  read, and the mint client acquires ro-ancestor discharges for
  `volume-ro` assumes (#564–#566).
- Liveness accepts every bound state, not only `Live` (#568).
- New-volume bootstrap: identity establishment rides `coord-rw`,
  the import runs under an `Importing` record with the key
  destroyed at the flip (#571/#572).
- A hermetic mint-e2e harness proves the attested enrollment loop
  end-to-end (#573/#574).

### Mint crypto hardening

A sweep through the macaroon and TPC machinery (#575–#589):

- Attestation modes renamed to role names; the `ATTESTABLE`
  registry retired — the declared contract is the authority
  (#575/#576).
- A fresh `r` is drawn per third-party caveat (`derive_r`
  retired); the discharge-as-primary verification path removed;
  caveats are never read by membership (#579–#581).
- Wire/crypto typing: a `KeyRef` sum type replaces the `kid`
  sentinel space, MAC tags are held as `blake3::Hash` for
  constant-time comparison, discharges pair to TPCs by ticket
  identity not bundle order, and crypto custody is typed
  end-to-end (#582–#586).
- The TPC AEAD seal moves to ChaCha20-Poly1305; `DOMAIN` bumped
  to v6 (#587/#589).

### Demo roles and config shape

Four demo roles collapse to two covering every namespace; the
auth and attestation planes nest under `[auth]` / `[attestation]`;
the client fails fast when `--attest` is missing for an attested
role (#590/#591/#593).

### Mint extracted to its own repository

The in-tree `mint/` subdirectory is removed — the attested-loop
e2e now consumes mint from its own repo, with the discharge
vectors guarded against cross-repo drift (#594).  Licensing
cleaned up: canonical Apache-2.0 text, MIT holder generalized to
"Elide Contributors" (#595).
